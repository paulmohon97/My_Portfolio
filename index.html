About Me

Hi, I’m Ananda Mohan Paul, an AI enthusiast passionate about turning complex algorithms into practical, real-world solutions. My journey into AI started with a simple curiosity: how can machines learn to understand human language? That curiosity grew into a deeper focus on Natural Language Processing and Large Language Models, and it’s driven my exploration of AI ever since.

Over time, I’ve become especially interested in areas like misinformation detection, fraud detection, information extraction and retrieval, Reinforcement Learning, Generative AI, and AI for Cybersecurity. These challenges are at the heart of building more trustworthy AI—making sure the models we create are reliable, fair, and able to make sense of messy, real-world data. Whether it’s training models to spot subtle patterns in text or developing systems that can extract valuable insights from large datasets, I love working on projects that blend cutting-edge research with practical impact. My hands-on experience as a System Engineer has taught me the importance of building robust and scalable systems. Still, my true passion lies in using AI to tackle complex problems that have a real impact, including applications in AI for Science.

I’m always excited about new opportunities to collaborate, share ideas, and push the boundaries of what AI can achieve—one project at a time.
